Column Definitions
Identification & Source

Formulation ID: Unique ID (e.g., Azizi_2025_Mg_2.0)
Author & Year: First author + year (e.g., Azizi 2025)
DOI: Digital Object Identifier

Composition - Oxidizer

AP (wt%): Ammonium perchlorate weight percent
AP Part Size (μm): AP particle size in micrometers

Composition - Catalyst

Catalyst Type: None | Mg | Fe2O3 | CuO | NiO | Co3O4 | Fe5B | CoC2O4 | CuCr2O4 | etc.
Catalyst (wt%): Catalyst weight percent
Catalyst Part Size (μm): Catalyst particle size

Composition - Binder

Binder Type: HTPB | PBAN | CTPB | Polyurethane | Sorbitol | Other
Binder (wt%): Binder weight percent

Composition - Additional Fuel

Fuel Type: Al | None | Other
Fuel (wt%): Fuel weight percent
Fuel Part Size (μm): Fuel particle size

Composition - Calculated

O/F Ratio: Oxidizer-to-fuel ratio
Total (wt%): Sum check (should = 100%)

Experimental Conditions

Init Temp (K): Initial temperature (default: 298)
Chamb Pres (MPa): Chamber pressure in megapascals
Chamb Pres (psi): Chamber pressure in psi (default: 1000)
Expans Rt: Expansion ratio (ε)
Ambient Pres (atm): Ambient/exit pressure (default: 1)

Primary Performance Metrics

Isp Meas (s): Measured specific impulse in seconds
Isp StdDev (s): Standard deviation of Isp
HoC (kJ/kg): Heat of combustion
HoC StdDev (kJ/kg): Standard deviation of HoC
Comb Temp (K): Combustion temperature
Tcomb StdDev (K): Standard deviation of combustion temp

Secondary Performance Metrics

Burn Rate (mm/s): Linear burning rate
Burn Rate Pres (MPa): Pressure at which burning rate measured
Pressure Exp (n): Pressure exponent (Saint-Robert's law)
*c (m/s)**: Characteristic velocity

Thermal Analysis Data (DSC/TGA)

LTD Onset (°C): Low-temp decomposition onset temperature
LTD Peak (°C): LTD peak temperature
LTD Heat (J/g): Heat release during LTD
HTD Onset (°C): High-temp decomposition onset temperature
HTD Peak (°C): HTD peak temperature
HTD Heat (J/g): Heat release during HTD
Total Mass Loss (%): Total mass loss percentage
Ea (kJ/mol): Activation energy

Data Quality & Metadata

Replicates: Number of test replicates
Test Method: Motor_Test | Strand_Burner | Bomb_Calorimetry | DSC/TGA | Other
Std Conditions: Y | N | Partial (standardized conditions?)
Assumed Values: List assumptions (e.g., "Pres=1000psi, T=298K")
Unit Conversions: Note conversions made
Derived Values: Note calculated values (e.g., "O/F from wt%")
Confidence: High | Medium | Low
Notes: Free text for important details
